Thank you for installing Visual Studio Codename Orcas Trial Edition.

Trial editions are valid for 90 or 180 days only. Once the 90 or 180 days has passed, you must obtain a valid Visual Studio Codename Orcas Professional Edition product key to continue using the product.

Product keys can be obtained in two ways: purchase Visual Studio Codename Orcas Professional Edition at a retail store or order a Visual Studio Codename Orcas Professional Edition product key online at http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkId=48753.

To upgrade the Trial Edition:
  1. On the Start menu, choose Control Panel.
  2. In the Control Panel, choose Add or Remove Programs.
  3. Choose Microsoft Visual Studio Codename Orcas Professional and then click Change/Remove.
  4. On the Visual Studio Codename Orcas Maintenance screen, enter the product key in the section Upgrade to Visual Studio Codename Orcas Professional and then click Upgrade.

Note: Product keys are found on the sleeve of the DVD or CD, or if you ordered a product key online, sent to you in email.
